The Client agrees to provide timely feedback, assets, and platform authorizations exactly when required by the Consultant to maintain velocity. Either party may unilaterally elect to terminate this arrangement with thirty (30) days’ written notice should the other party fundamentally breach the terms laid forth without immediate cure. If terminated by the Client before Phase 2 begins, the remainder of any pending block retainers automatically accelerates and becomes immediately payable.
